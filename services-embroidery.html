Order your custom embroidered uniforms here!

At Smile Kiddie Shop, we personally embroider your school logos onto shirts, sweaters, and blazers! We also offer personalized names and custom designs as well. Your orders can be completed either on the order form below, or if you have any additional requests, you can also order over the phone or through e-mail.

If you would like to do a custom design or personalized name of your own, please e-mail us with an image of the design in mind for a free quote.

Delivery through USPS/UPS is also available, but a shipping fee will be charged.

For all inquiries, do not hesitate to visit our store location or contact us by any means. All contact means can be found on our Contact Us page!
